PESAMATRIX V2 - CLOUD TO CLOUD COPY TRADING PLATFORM

STAGE 1: SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE

Build a production-grade cloud-to-cloud copy trading platform using MetaApi CopyFactory.

Mandatory Requirements

- No Redis.
- No MT5 terminals running on Replit.
- No VPS.
- No trade copier EAs.
- No local MT5 terminals.
- No signal bridge software.

Required Architecture

User MT5 Account
↓
MetaApi Account
↓
MetaApi CopyFactory
↓
Subscriber MT5 Accounts

All trade copying must be handled by MetaApi CopyFactory.

Replit only manages:

- User accounts
- Authentication
- Subscriptions
- Payments
- Strategy management
- Subscriber management
- Reporting

Replit must never process or relay individual trade events.

selected_days × daily_fee

Display amount before payment.

---

STAGE 6: TRADING DAYS RULE

Trading days are:

Monday
Tuesday
Wednesday
Thursday
Friday

Non-trading days:

Saturday
Sunday

Subscription countdown only decreases on trading days.

Example:

User buys 5 trading days on Friday.

Remaining days:

Friday = Day 1

Weekend ignored

Monday = Day 2
Tuesday = Day 3
Wednesday = Day 4
Thursday = Day 5

Subscription expires after Thursday market close.
